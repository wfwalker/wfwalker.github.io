In [this post entitled "FrequencyReducesDifficulty"](http://martinfowler.com/bliki/FrequencyReducesDifficulty.html "Frequency Reduces Difficulty"), Martin Fowler explains why doing painful things more often reduces the pain. Think pushups, cleaning the catbox, refactoring ugly code, and integrating multiple subsystems.
