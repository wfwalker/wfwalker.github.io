[Morten](http://www.morkeleb.com/about/ "about Morten")'s presentation is a hilarious, compelling fable of what happens when a .NET shop relying on manual processes becomes a victim of its own success and ends up with an exploding number of software versions in the field. The fable ends happily using [Hudson](http://hudson-ci.org/) and [Jenkins](http://jenkins-ci.org/) for repeatable, automated deployments. Whew.
