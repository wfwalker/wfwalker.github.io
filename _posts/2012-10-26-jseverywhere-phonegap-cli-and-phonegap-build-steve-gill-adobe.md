Steve Gill is demoing "Cordova Client", in which you have command-line tools to build, deploy, and manage Cordova-based applications on Blackberry, Android, and iOS. This is still in beta. It's at [github.com/filmaj/cordova-client](http://github.com/filmaj/cordova-client "cordova-client"). It requires Node.js, should be available in npm.
