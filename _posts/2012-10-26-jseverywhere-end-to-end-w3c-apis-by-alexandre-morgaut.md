Alexandre is giving a great overview of the history of JavaScript, popular API's, and the role of W3C and other standards bodies. He gives a shoutout [to Kevin Dangoor for CommonJS](http://www.blueskyonmars.com/2010/01/29/commonjs-the-first-year/ "Kevin's blog on CommonJS"), then proposes the idea that remote CommonJS processes can be thought of as remote Web Workers. Wakanda makes big use of CommonJS.
