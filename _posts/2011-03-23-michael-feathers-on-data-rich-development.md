In his recent post ["Data-Rich Development"](http://michaelfeathers.typepad.com/michael_feathers_blog/2011/03/data-rich-development.html "Data-Rich Development"), Michael Feathers talks about supplementing the recent emphasis on incremental design through TDD with a longer-term perspective gained through analyzing changes to the codebase over time. As a budding source code miner myself, I was heartened to learn that he's thinking similar thoughts.
